Quesadillas

The delicious cousin of the main mexican dish

Ingredients

  • Any kind of tortillas.
  • Any cheese that melts
  • (Optional) Any grilled meat

Steps

  1. Warm one side of the flour tortillas on a skillet.
  2. Add cheese into the tortilla.
  3. Add any protein or vegteable that suits you well on top of the cheese.
  4. fold the tortilla into a half.
  5. Add salsa to taste.
  6. Bite and enjoy!
