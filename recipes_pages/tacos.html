Tacos

The signature recipe of the Mexican country

Ingredients

  • Corn tortillas
  • Grilled meat (e.g., beef, chicken, or pork)
  • Sliced onions(optional)
  • Cilantro
  • Lime wedges
  • Salsa (red or green)

Instructions

  1. Warm the corn tortillas on a skillet.
  2. Add grilled meat to the tortilla.
  3. Top with sliced onions and cilantro.
  4. Squeeze lime juice over the taco.
  5. Add salsa to taste.
  6. Fold and enjoy!
