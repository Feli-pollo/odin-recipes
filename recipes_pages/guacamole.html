Guacamole

The signature companion of all mexican food

Ingredients

  • Avocado
  • Lime juice
  • (Optional) Sliced onions
  • (Optional) Sliced tomatoes
  • (Optional) Milk
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Mash the avocado until have the desired texture.
  2. (Optional) Add a little sprink of milk for a more liquid consistence.
  3. (Optional) Add the sliced onions and tomatoes.
  4. Squeeze lime juice over at taste.
  5. Mix everything together.
  6. And enjoy!
